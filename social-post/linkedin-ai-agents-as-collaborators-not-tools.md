"Have your agent call my agent" sounds like a punchline. It's actually an architecture.

Your agent knows what you know. Mine knows what I know. The protocol between them connects our expertise without either of us losing control.

It's based on how effective teams actually work: they don't share all knowledge—they share awareness of *who knows what*. Agents become carriers of that awareness, coordinating on your behalf.

The future isn't tools or overlords. It's agents as genuine participants in shared ecosystems of knowledge and trust.
