# Good People Are Not Enough

---

## A collective does not become collaborative just because good people are in the same room

---

## Rizom is not a traditional team

- more freedom
- more emergence
- more possibility

---

## More possibility, but also

- more ambiguity
- more invisibility
- more dependence on chance

---

## Teams work because people know who knows what

---

## In Rizom, too much stays invisible

- what someone brings
- what they are looking for
- what they are working on
- where collaboration might exist

---

## The bottleneck is not talent. The bottleneck is legibility

---

## Rizom Brains can help the collective become more knowable to itself

---

## Legibility first. Coordination next

- Brains make collaboration easier to see
- agent-to-agent interaction could make it easier to activate

---

## The question is not whether Rizom has good people

---

## The question is whether collaboration can find us
