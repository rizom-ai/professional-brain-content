# New Institutions

## Description
Explore how our institutions are failing us and what comes next. This series examines the social contracts that actually hold communities together, the design principles for organizations that can adapt to complexity, and the practical work of building new structures that work like living systems rather than machines. Discover why traditional institutional models are breaking down and what we can learn from decentralization, regenerative design, and ecosystem thinking to create something genuinely inhabitable.
