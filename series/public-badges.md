# Public Badges

## Description
Public Badges is a series about how digital credentials can do more than verify skills—they can express values, build trust, and strengthen communities. Through topics like value-driven development and the fundamentals of trust, it offers practical insight into designing badge systems that are meaningful, credible, and built for real-world use.
