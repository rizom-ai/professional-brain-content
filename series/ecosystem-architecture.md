# Ecosystem Architecture

## Description
Ecosystem Architecture explores how organizations and communities can be designed as living systems rather than machines—drawing from philosophy, biology, decentralization, and cultural innovation to build structures that actually work. Through essays spanning regenerative design, organizational theory, and technological futures, this series reveals the principles and practices needed to create spaces where people and systems can genuinely thrive. Whether you're building something new or reimagining what already exists, these pieces offer both the conceptual foundation and practical wisdom for designing the future.
