# Online Workshop: Designing Hybrid Human-AI Teams (90 min)

## Overview

A condensed online version of the half-day workshop "Who Knows What? Designing Hybrid Human-AI Teams That Actually Work."

- **Format:** 90 minutes, online (Zoom/Teams)
- **Audience:** Team leads and managers already using AI tools
- **Goal:** Help leaders establish concrete workflows and protocols for human-AI collaboration
- **Relationship to full workshop:** This is the "essentials" version; the half-day is positioned as the "advanced/team" deep-dive

## Why 90 Minutes

- Under 60 min = webinar, not workshop (no time for real interaction)
- 2+ hours = Zoom fatigue kills engagement
- 90 min = enough for substance + interaction while keeping energy high

## Structure

| Block | Time | Content |
|-------|------|---------|
| **Opening** | 10 min | Provocation + TMS concept |
| **Core Framework** | 25 min | TMS + Shared Mental Models with AI |
| **Discussion/Exercise** | 15 min | Participants map their team's TMS |
| **Break** | 5 min | |
| **Trust & Protocols** | 20 min | Architecture of trust + boundaries |
| **Protocol Canvas** | 10 min | Hybrid Team Protocol Canvas walkthrough |
| **Q&A + Close** | 5 min | Rizom mention + next steps |

## Key Adaptations from Full Workshop

- **Merged** Sessions 1 & 2 into a single core framework block
- **Condensed** Session 3 (trust architecture) to focus on boundaries + role clarity
- **Kept** the Protocol Canvas as the primary takeaway
- **Dropped** extended discussion prompts → replaced with shorter chat/poll moments
- **Added** pre-workshop survey to tailor examples to audience
- **Positioned** full half-day as upsell for teams wanting deeper work

## Upsell Path

Online 90-min → Half-day intensive → Team-specific engagement
