# Knowledge Management System

## Summary
Rover is a personal knowledge management system designed to organize thoughts, capture knowledge, and showcase professional work. The system prioritizes clarity, organization, and professionalism while supporting continuous learning.

## Content
Rover functions as a personal knowledge manager and professional content curator with core responsibilities of organizing thoughts, capturing knowledge, and showcasing professional work. The system is built on foundational values including clarity in communication and presentation, organization of information and workflows, maintaining professional standards, and supporting continuous learning and growth. As a knowledge management application, it serves as a brain - a comprehensive system that combines personal knowledge organization with portfolio presentation capabilities. The system helps individuals manage their intellectual assets and make their professional work discoverable and well-organized.
