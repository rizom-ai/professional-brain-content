Innovation is reframed as an ongoing practice rather than a product or artifact. The content argues that true innovation is not about creating singular breakthrough technologies, but about fostering a sustained culture of ideation, experimentation, and continuous refinement. Practice innovation emphasizes collaboration, adaptability, and resilience, seeking solutions that are sustainable and equitable. This approach aligns with regenerative design, where waste and imperfection are seen as seeds for future growth rather than failures. The focus shifts from extracting value through efficiency and scale to cultivating value through meaningful, context-sensitive interventions. The methodology highlights the importance of iteration, community involvement, and the creation of systems that are capable of renewal and transformation, rather than simply maintaining the status quo.
