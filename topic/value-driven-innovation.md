A recurring theme is the idea of innovation as an ongoing practice rooted in explicit values rather than a one-time artifact or product. This includes the development of ethical frameworks, such as Public Badges for measurable value commitments, and methodologies like Value Driven Development that make values explicit in the software development process. The content critiques dominant innovation mindsets driven by scarcity or excess, advocating instead for regenerative, sustainable, and inclusive approaches. True innovation is framed as collaborative, adaptive, and oriented toward the well-being of both communities and the environment. This theme connects technical practices (Open Badges, BDD/TDD in software, regenerative design) with broader philosophical and ethical questions about what it means to build and sustain meaningful, equitable futures.
