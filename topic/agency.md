---
title: Agency
---
Agency is the philosophical core of the content on bots, collaboration, and intelligence. One post explicitly argues that agency matters more than humanness in teamwork, especially in hybrid human-machine environments. Another line of thought treats intelligence itself as a practice of purposeful action rather than a passive service. Across these works, agents are evaluated by what they contribute, how they participate, and whether they can be trusted to act meaningfully within a system. This reframes AI from a tool-centered paradigm into one of participation and responsibility. Agency becomes the decisive criterion for collaboration, governance, and organizational design.
