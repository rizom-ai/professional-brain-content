# Ecosystem Architecture & Alternative Futures

## Summary
The article proposes ecosystem architecture as a practice for designing multiple inhabitable futures based on shared values, rejecting predetermined dystopian narratives. It emphasizes the importance of alignment before connection, learning from past futurisms, and creating alternatives that allow people to choose between different possible worlds rather than accepting a single inevitable outcome.

## Content
