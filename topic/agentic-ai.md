---
title: Agentic AI
---
Agentic AI is explored as the shift from passive tools to active participants in work and collaboration. Several pieces describe bots and AI agents that can hold knowledge, coordinate tasks, and act with some degree of autonomy. The emphasis is on agency as the meaningful criterion, not humanness or artificiality. This perspective shapes the design of collaborative systems where humans and agents share roles in knowledge work. It also raises governance concerns around autonomy, reputation, trust, and accountability. The content consistently treats agentic AI as part of a broader ecosystem of collaboration rather than as a standalone intelligence layer.
