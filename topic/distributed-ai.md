---
title: Distributed AI
---
The content also develops a vision of distributed AI made up of many narrow, specialized agents instead of one universal system. It argues that solving real organizational problems is better than chasing moonshots or general intelligence fantasies. These agents are designed to handle focused tasks such as onboarding, knowledge retrieval, message synchronization, and summarization. The approach emphasizes human-in-the-loop workflows, modularity, and practical automation over hype. This topic overlaps with collaboration and knowledge infrastructure, but its core is the strategic use of small AI systems working together.
