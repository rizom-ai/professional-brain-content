CastYourArt presents itself as a media library focused on videos and films about art, with navigation to the home page, media library, watchlist, login, and company information. The homepage highlights recent featured posts and exhibition portraits such as Kiteezi, Haakon Neubert, Richard Prince, Im erweiterten Raum, KAWS, and Canaletto & Bellotto, each paired with video playback and watchlist options.

The site emphasizes that members gain access to over 600 inspiring films on contemporary and historical art and can create and share playlists. It also describes full-service audio and video production for artists, galleries, museums, and other cultural institutions, and provides contact details, newsletter signup, and additional links such as podcast, press, imprint, privacy, and help.
