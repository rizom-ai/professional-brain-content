The page presents RDevelopment’s flagship initiative, the AI Datacampus Rotterdam, as a pioneering “living-learning-working” ecosystem located in StadionDriehoek in Rotterdam-Zuid. It is described as a major engine for talent development and technological innovation that connects education, industry, and community while supporting the growth of artificial intelligence and emerging technology sectors across Europe.

The content explains how the campus is meant to be highly integrated, with student and young professional housing, educational facilities across MBO/HBO/university levels, technology labs, innovation studios, hospitality and leisure amenities, mobility services, and public green spaces. It emphasizes public-private collaboration, sustainability, adaptability, and the goal of strengthening Rotterdam-Zuid while creating inclusive economic growth and preventing brain drain through local opportunities for trained talent.
