This page is a Dutch partner brief for Numbers Protocol’s planned participation in Eureka GlobalStars Taiwan 2026. It explains that a Dutch and Taiwanese partner can jointly apply for bilateral R&D cash grants, with each side funded locally by its own government, and highlights key program details such as the deadline (2026-09-15), funding limits, duration, themes, and award timing.

The brief proposes five possible collaboration directions, prioritizing Trustworthy AI Content Provenance, Digital Product Passport, and Omni Edge trustworthy edge AI hardware. It also outlines the funding structure, next steps for confirming eligibility and drafting an LOI, a pre-discussion checklist for potential Dutch partners, and a contact email for follow-up. It is explicitly presented as a structured proposal based on a Google Doc and notes that final submission should be verified with the relevant agencies.
