Robin van Persie is a Dutch football coach and former professional footballer from Rotterdam, best known for his prolific goal scoring as a striker for Feyenoord, Arsenal, Manchester United, Fenerbahçe, and the Netherlands national team. The article outlines his development from youth football at Excelsior and Feyenoord into one of the best strikers of his generation, highlighting major milestones such as his Premier League success with Arsenal and Manchester United, his transfer to Fenerbahçe, and his return to Feyenoord before retirement.

The page also details his international career, including appearances at the 2006, 2010, and 2014 FIFA World Cups and UEFA Euro 2008 and 2012, where he captained the Netherlands and became the country’s all-time top scorer. It further covers his transition into coaching with Feyenoord and Heerenveen, plus personal life, playing style, career statistics, honours, and records.
