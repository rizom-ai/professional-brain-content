42workspace is Rotterdam's leading coworking space dedicated to innovative tech startups, scale-ups, and freelancers. Located in the heart of Rotterdam, it provides flexible membership options ranging from day passes (€20) to private offices (€375/month), along with comprehensive facilities including high-speed internet, meeting rooms, a fully equipped gym, 24/7 access, and a rooftop event space. Beyond physical workspace, 42workspace fosters an active tech community through a community app, networking events, and collaboration opportunities. The facility actively supports Rotterdam's tech ecosystem by partnering with innovation hubs, investors, and local authorities, and hosts a variety of events including meetups, tech talks, pitch competitions, and hackathons.
