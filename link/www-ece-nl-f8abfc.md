The Erasmus Centre for Entrepreneurship homepage presents the organization as a partner in “StartingUp” and showcases its main areas of activity, including education and training, insights, international work, and about information. It features upcoming events, featured programs such as ELITE and Orchestrating Innovation, and a research section with reports like the Top 250 Insights Report 2026, Code-V Data Report 2026, and European Scaleup Monitor 2026.

The page also promotes a book project with HelloPrint called “Founders Frustrations,” highlights a broad set of partner organizations, and invites visitors to subscribe to the newsletter or follow the centre on social media. The footer includes contact guidance, legal links, and cookie/privacy policy information, indicating a professional institutional website focused on entrepreneurship and ecosystem development.
