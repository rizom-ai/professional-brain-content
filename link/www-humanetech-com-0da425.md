The page presents the Center for Humane Technology’s newly launched Emerging Voices in AI and Society Fellowship, a six-month pilot program designed to bring in three mid-career fellows with interdisciplinary expertise in the impacts of technology on society. The fellowship is remote, part-time, and offers a $30,000 stipend tied to three milestones, with an application deadline of July 12, 2026.

It outlines the kinds of applicants CHT wants—researchers, policy analysts, journalists, advocates, and tech insiders—along with expectations such as producing a flagship research output, writing op-eds, appearing on a podcast, and participating in CHT’s media ecosystem. The page also details benefits, application requirements, FAQ information, and CHT’s emphasis on public communication, nuanced thinking about AI harms, and alignment with its framing of technology’s societal impacts.
