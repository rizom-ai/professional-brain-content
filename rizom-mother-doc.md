# Rizom Mother Doc

## 1. Core Identity (Internal)

### What Rizom Is

Rizom is a distributed consultancy that can do what traditional consultancies cannot: mobilize specialized expertise in hours instead of months, without the knowledge loss and coordination overhead that normally comes with flexible teams.

We are not a staffing agency. We are not a freelancer marketplace. We are a network of independent professionals with shared methodology and AI infrastructure that makes us operate like a single, adaptive organism.

### What We Believe

- The future of knowledge work is distributed, not centralized
- The bottleneck in most organizations is not talent: it is knowledge flow
- AI should augment human collaboration, not replace it
- Professionals should own what they build

### Why We Exist

Traditional organizations trap knowledge in two places: key people's heads and scattered tools. This makes scaling painful and makes organizations dependent on individuals rather than systems. We are building the alternative.


## 2. The Problem (Shared)

Teams and organizations fail to scale. The root cause is almost always poor knowledge sharing.

Knowledge gets trapped in:
- **People** — unofficial "go-to people" who become bottlenecks
- **Tools** — old Slack threads, forgotten docs, private AI chats

This creates predictable failure patterns:
- New hires ask the same questions repeatedly, interrupting busy teammates
- Important context is inaccessible when decisions need to be made
- When domain experts are unavailable or leave, projects grind to a halt
- Coordination costs grow faster than the team


## 3. Our Approach (Internal)

### The TMS Framework

Our methodology is grounded in Transactive Memory Systems (TMS), an organizational psychology framework that explains how high-performing teams actually work.

The core insight: effective teams do not share all knowledge, they share awareness of *who knows what*, and build trust and systems to access it quickly.

TMS has three dimensions:

| Dimension | What it means | What breaks when it's weak |
|-----------|---------------|----------------------------|
| **Specialization** | Knowledge is distributed, not duplicated. People know who knows what. | Everyone tries to know everything, or nobody knows who to ask. |
| **Credibility** | Team members trust each other's expertise. | Second-guessing, redundant verification, slow decisions. |
| **Coordination** | The right knowledge reaches the right person at the right time. | Bottlenecks, delays, context loss. |

### How the Pieces Fit Together

**The Network** is the product. Access to high-performing distributed professionals who can mobilize fast around client outcomes.

**The AI Agents** (Rover, Recall, Ranger) are the infrastructure that makes the network operationally viable. They solve the knowledge-sharing problem at scale—maintaining specialization maps, building credibility signals, and enabling coordination across a distributed team.

**The Methodology** (TMS-informed practices, playbooks, contracts) is the intellectual property that shapes both how the network operates and how the agents are designed.

This is what lets us deliver something clients could not previously buy: outcome-based work from distributed teams without the usual overhead.


## 4. What We Offer (External-Ready)

### For Organizations

**Product Teams** — Outcome-based engagements where a Rizom team owns a problem, not just hours. Fast ramp-up, shared context, no knowledge loss when the engagement ends.

**Workshops & Consultancy** — Help your existing team diagnose and fix knowledge-sharing breakdowns using the TMS framework.

**Playbooks & Contracts** — Structures for working with distributed professionals without the usual coordination tax.

### For Independent Professionals

**The Network** — Access to meaningful work with other high-performers. Shared methodology means you can plug into teams without lengthy onboarding.

**Events & Formats** — Connections that go deeper than LinkedIn. Formats designed for trust-building, not just networking.

**Shared Infrastructure** — AI tools and systems that handle the operational overhead of independent work.
